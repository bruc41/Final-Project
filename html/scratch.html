My Top 5 Beaches on Oahu, Hawai'i

  1. Pounders Beach, Oahu, Hawai'i
    • Famous for fun shorebreak, perfect barrels

  2. Sandy Beach, Oahu, Hawai'i
    • Powerful shorebreak, strong current, local crowds
  3. Makapu'u, Oahu, Hawai'i
    • Powerful shorebreak, heavy barrels, thick waves
  4. Waimea Bay, Oahu, Hawai'i
    • Massive winter waves
  5. Pipeline, Oahu, Hawai'i
    • Famous for huge, perfect barrels
